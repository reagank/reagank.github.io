Planning and Setbacks

2018-05-16

At the end of last week, I was able to view the Kinect video & depth streams, and drive the bot using a bluetooth controller. That basically meant I’d made a very complicated (and expensive) R/C car. I started to focus on building the software, and in particular object tracking. Then, the Kinect stopped working. I believe I fried it - I tried connecting it to other computers, but didn’t get anywhere. I’ve ordered a replacement, and it’ll be here by next week.

In the mean time, I’ve been thinking about what the end goals are. I’ve come up with a few:

  • I want it to be able to recognize a rectangle of a specific color, and to keep it within a certain distance, rolling forward and back based on the distance the Kinect registers.

  • I want it to respond to a voice command, finding a person and driving toward the them when it hears “Come here”

    So I’m going to work on some fit and finish parts of the robot until I’m able to work with the Kinect again. I’ve loc-tited the nuts on the axles, and made sure the bolts are tight and straight. I’m also going to mount the components more securely in the box. I’m hoping that by the end of next week I’ll be able to track objects, and I’ll post more photos then.
